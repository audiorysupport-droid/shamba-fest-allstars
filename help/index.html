Help & Support

Find configuration guidance, policy details, and answers to frequently asked questions.

How do I purchase a ticket on ShambaFest?
Simply navigate to our upcoming events portal via the header navigation links, pick your preferred holiday package or local event experience, and select your payment checkout options. Your electronic pass configuration parameters will be immediate.
What are the fees for listing and selling event admissions?
Creating an account and putting together your listing parameters is completely free. ShambaFest implements a competitive processing service calculation standard during payout transfers upon ticket purchase completions.
How are payouts dispatched back to organizers and merchants?
Payout funds accumulate internally within secure cloud structures connected directly with your merchant configuration values. Verified operational balances are systematically transferred straight into linked payment structures post event completions.
What is ShambaFest's standard refund arrangement model?
Refund guidelines vary specifically based on operational mandates configured by separate event organizers. Please review individual experience policy modules or get in touch with our team for resolution verification if cancellations occur.

Still need assistance with setup details?

Our unified service operations desk remains available to assist with custom configurations or merchant deployment support parameters.
